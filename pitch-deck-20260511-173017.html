In one line

We took the mechanics of a real-world credit fund, replaced the lawyers and Excel with two Solana programs, and turned the resulting risk layers into SPL tokens anyone can hold, trade, or build with.

For LPs

Pick your risk: stable yield, balanced, or high-upside. Deposit USDC, get tranche tokens. Yield accrues into the token's NAV; you exit any time at the current price — either through the vault or the AMM.

For borrowers

Apply, attach cross-chain collateral via IKA dWallets, get USDC disbursed on Solana. Repay in USDC or fiat through Dodo. Default risk is absorbed by the Alpha tranche first.

The stack · what we plug into & why

Five dependencies.
Each replacing months of work.

Solana · Anchor
Base layer for the credit engine and AMM. Sub-cent fees make per-LP NAV math affordable; Ed25519 precompile makes oracle verification one instruction.
prism_core · prism_amm
25/25
Anchor tests passing
5
Dependencies wired end-to-end
IKA Network
Borrowers lock native BTC or ETH in an IKA dWallet; oracle attests, Solana verifies, loan disburses.
Solves: cross-chain collateral without a custodial bridge
Encrypt
FHE oracle proves a loan is in default homomorphically — never reveals the borrower's balance sheet.
Solves: confidential default proofs for institutional credit
Cloak
Per-LP yield payouts route through a shielded batch; viewing keys let each LP audit only their slice.
Solves: privacy on cash flows without losing auditability
Dune SIM
Subscribes to every Anchor event — deposits, yield, swaps, losses — and feeds the live dashboard ticker.
Solves: real-time indexing without running our own subgraph
Dodo Payments
Borrowers repay in local fiat — UPI, cards, bank transfer. Dodo settles USDC into the vault and handles tax + compliance.
Solves: fiat on-ramp for real-world borrower repayment
